nuestra frontera/our border

book

Our Border/Nuestra Frontera narrates the contemporary culture and lifestyle of the U.S./MEXICO border region. The bookâ€™s structure is informed by the content, it is conformed of two books that meet in the middle in order to generate meaning. The exterior pages of the book narrate different characteristics of the border region in two different languages (english and spanish). While the middle pages must be placed in accordance to other pages in order to make sense of the meaning; emulating interdependence of this two countries.
