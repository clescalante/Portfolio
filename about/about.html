CYNTHIA ESCALANTE
multidisciplinary designer—u.s./mex.

Cynthia Escalante is a multidisciplinary designer, her work covers a wide range of areas in design, including urbanism, architecture and graphic design. Her practice wanders between the boundaries of graphic design and architecture and questions the role of graphic design in physical spaces and at urban scopes. Escalante was born in El Paso, TX and raised in Ciudad Juarez, CHIH. In 2016 she received her Bachelor of Science in Architecture from Texas Tech University College of Architecture in El Paso. She is currently studying to obtain her MFA in Graphic Design at California College of the Arts in San Francisco, CA.

Cynthia Escalante es una diseñadora multidisciplinaria, su trabajo cubre una amplia gama de áreas en diseño, incluyendo urbanismo, arquitectura y diseño gráfico. Escalante nació en El Paso, TX y creció en Ciudad Juárez, CHIH. En el 2016 recibió su Licenciatura en Ciencias en Arquitectura de Texas Tech University College of Architecture en El Paso.
