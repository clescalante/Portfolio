localize

urbanism

Localize provides suppor for local/small businesses owner who are closing down/having to relocate (pushed away) due to the current tech-driven development of the city. By taking advateage of Privately Owned Public Open Space (POPOS) that are emerging in the city, localize takes advatage of these spaces and adapts them to venues for events organized by small/local businesses.
